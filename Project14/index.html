The Pacific Northwest


Mount Rainier

Mt. Rainier National Park is a worldwide tourist destination for those itching to see the great beauty of the Pacific Northwest. The best time of year to visit is early to mid-summer to see the vibrant fields of alpine wildflowers. Hikes are easily accessible to the snow level in the summer time, with paved pathways reaching far enough for some of the best views.

Although some days can be crowded during the season, for many the views cannot compare to anywhere else. Personally, I think this is a must see location for the PNW, despite the amount of traffic that is seen. If you aren't experienced with hiking or who simply want to experience the great outdoors without worries, Mt. Rainier National Park may be the destination for you. Your only complaint might be the pesky mosquitoes that are prevalent during the summer season, but you quickly forget them as you take in the wonder that is Mt. Rainier.
